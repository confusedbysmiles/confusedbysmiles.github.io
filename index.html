About Me

I am a PhD student in Educational Studies at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, specializing in Innovative Learning Technologies with a certificate in Mixed Methods Research. My research explores the intersection of technology, education, and inclusive learning environments.

My academic journey is driven by a passion for understanding how emerging technologies can transform educational experiences and create more accessible and equitable learning opportunities for diverse populations.

Research Focus

My research examines how innovative technologies can be leveraged to create more accessible and effective learning environments. I employ mixed methods approaches to gain comprehensive insights into the complex dynamics of technology-enhanced learning spaces.

Research interests include:

  • AI integration in educational contexts
  • Creating student-centered learning environments
  • Developing innovative tools for inclusive education
  • Data-driven approaches to educational technology
  • Social-emotional learning through technology
